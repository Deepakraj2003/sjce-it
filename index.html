Ethical Hacking:-

HebeSec Technologies Private Limited provides cyber security workshops to school students. In this Ethical Hacking Training course, Students will learn about Networking Concepts, the Basics of Cyber Security, Methodologies, and Techniques used in Ethical Hacking.


Guidelines:

  • Before Starting the class, Non - Disclosure Agreement (NDA) should be Filled out and signed by the candidates.

  • Classes will be in Live Practical and Theoretical.

  • Certificates will be given only after completion of the documentation and reports.

  • Students must have a laptop or can attend the class through College Lab.


Training Includes:

  • Installation of Ethical Hacking tools

  • Participation Certificate
